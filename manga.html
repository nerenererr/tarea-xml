Nana Komatsu seeks love and happiness, while Nana Osaki pursues her dream of becoming a famous rock musician. ベルセルク

Genre: Seinen
Author: Kentaro Miura †
Year: 1989
The dark epic follows Guts, a mercenary swordsman wielding the legendary Dragonslayer. Cursed with the Brand of Sacrifice, which attracts demons, he seeks revenge against Griffith, his former closest friend and leader of the Band of the Hawk, who betrayed him during the ritual known as the Eclipse. A masterpiece that explores the limits of humanity in a brutal medieval fantasy world.
